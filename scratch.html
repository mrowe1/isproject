Theres Nothing Quite Like Cars

Beauty, Passion, & Engineering

I love Cars, I love the way they look, the way they sound, and they way they feel. I love the feeling of knowing the machine is only as good as you. I didn't start really getting into cars until I turned 16, thats when it started to feel like something that I could spend thousands of hours doing and have zero problem with it. I hope this page doesn't only show you skills I have learned with HTML and CSS but I hope I can also show you why cars are so fascinating and beautiful to me.


My Favorite Cars

  1. McLaren P1
    • Hybrid Hypercar
    • 903 horsepower
    • 0-60 mph in 2.8 seconds
    • In my opinion the most beautiful car ever created, this thing is pristine engineering and the love of cars wrapped up into the most perfect machine I have ever seen. The way McLaren designed the shape of the car to direct the air through and around the body letting all 3075 lbs of perfection glide through the air is simply stunning.
  2. McLaren 720S
    • Another testament to McLaren's beautiful touch and design
    • 720 horsepower
    • 0-60 in 2.9 seconds
    • McLaren just does it right, two similar but starkly different instances that validate the quality of their craft at McLaren. Extremely similar to the P1 in many of its specs yet comparing them is like comparing paintings by an artist.
  3. Porsche 911 GT3RS
    • The Gt3RS is another gorgeous piece of machinery
    • 518 horsepower (base model)
    • 0-60 in 3.0 seconds
    • My attratction to this car definitely comes from the after market scene, so many of these cars are customized by their buyers to be more personalized and look absolutely stunning.
  4. Tesla Model S Plaid
    • When this car came out it was the fastest 0-60 production car in the world.
    • 1,020 horsepower
    • 0-60 in 1.99 seconds
    • The most beautiful thing about cars is there are so many ways to "do it right". The Model S being an electric car, is able to achieve a higher acceleration than gas cars. There are so many trade-offs for gas versus electric but either way the engineering and precision that goes into these cars is astounding.
  5. Tesla Roadster
    • Still a concept car by Tesla set for 2026
    • horsepower is still unknown as the car is set to release in roughly a year from now
    • 0-60 in 1.9 seconds
    • I love that cars become a goal to accomplish, the engineering pushes us further and further faster and faster and that keeps us evolving as humans. Humans need evolution and so we see people try and push concepts and goals to their absolute limits. It is totally inspiring to me.

History

McLaren was founded by New Zealand race car designer, driver, engineer, and inventor Bruce McLaren in 1963. I find him inspiring because he lived and breathed cars, that was the thing he pursued and he made it work.
